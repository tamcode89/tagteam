Welcome to The Tag Team Project

Helping individuals and families build a stable, purpose-driven life.

The Tag Team Project is a nonprofit organization committed to connecting people with safe housing, supportive services, and community resources that encourage healing, growth, and long-term success.

We primarily serve individuals facing substance abuse challenges, people with disabilities, veterans, and women with children through compassionate, faith-based care.

Explore Our Services Get in Touch

Our Purpose

To help individuals achieve stability and self-sufficiency by connecting them with essential resources, support services, and safe housing in a caring, faith-based environment.

  • Safe and stable housing
  • Mental health and recovery support
  • Connections to daily living and long-term resources

Who We Support

We are dedicated to serving people who need encouragement, resources, and a strong support system.

Mission Statement

To provide faith-based support that empowers individuals and families to achieve stability, recovery, and self-sufficiency through housing, resources, and compassionate care.

Vision Statement

We envision a community where every individual—regardless of their past or circumstances—has access to safe housing, supportive services, and the opportunity to build a stable, purpose-driven life.
