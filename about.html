About Us

Built on faith, compassion, and the belief that every life can be restored.

The Tag Team Project exists to help individuals and families overcome barriers and move toward a stable, healthy, and purpose-driven future through faith-based support and practical resources.

Mission Statement

To provide faith-based support that empowers individuals and families to achieve stability, recovery, and self-sufficiency through housing, resources, and compassionate care.

Vision Statement

We envision a community where every individual—regardless of their past or circumstances—has access to safe housing, supportive services, and the opportunity to build a stable, purpose-driven life.

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by values that reflect our heart for service and transformation.

Faith & Compassion – We serve with love, grace, and understanding.
Dignity & Respect – Every person is treated with worth and honor.
Accountability – We encourage responsibility and personal growth.
Community – We build strong support systems and connections.
Empowerment – We equip individuals with tools for long-term success.

Why Our Work Matters

We believe people thrive when they have both practical support and compassionate encouragement. By helping connect clients to housing, recovery support, and community resources, we aim to strengthen lives, restore hope, and create lasting change.
